Research Summary

I work at the intersection of reinforcement learning and computational neuroscience, focusing on modeling brain-inspired decision-making and designing closed-loop therapeutic systems. My work spans deep RL, offline RL, and multi-agent systems, with applications in Parkinson’s disease, glucose regulation, and physiological control under delayed rewards and uncertainty.
