About

I work at the intersection of reinforcement learning and computational neuroscience, focusing on how brain-inspired decision-making can be modeled and leveraged for clinical interventions.

My research spans deep RL, offline RL, and closed-loop neural control, with applications in Parkinson’s disease, glucose regulation, and multi-agent physiological systems.
